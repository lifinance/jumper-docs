## The tech Powering Jumper Earn

### The Discovery Engine

**Jumper Earn** features 6**00+ opportunities on 10+ chains** enough to overwhelm even seasoned users. That is why we have a way to help you focus on the best opportunities.

Each time a user visits Jumper Earn, we:

- **Scan their portfolio** to detect idle assets and current DeFi positions
- **Analyze** these positions alongside **live market data**
- **Score** each opportunity based on how relevant and advantageous it is for that specific user

This scoring mechanism allows us to **handpick personalized recommendations** i.e. the ones that truly fit the user's risk profile, liquidity preferences, and yield potential.

**Example:**

A user holds **ETH on Base** and an **USDC vault position on Euler (Arbitrum)**.

We'd assign:

- ✅ A **bonus** to low-risk opportunities (large money market pools, conservative strategies)
- ✅ A **bonus** to opportunities on liquid chains (Arbitrum, Base, Ethereum)
- ✅ A **bonus** to opportunities tied to ETH or USDC
- ✅ A **bonus** to opportunities offering higher realized APR than the user's current vault
- ✅ A **bonus** to opportunities with active reward campaigns

## How are opportunities scored?

Each opportunity receives a relevance score based on multiple factors, including:

- Risk profile alignment
- Chain liquidity
- Asset compatibility
- Relative yield performance
- Reward incentives

### Example scoring logic

If a user holds:

- ETH on Base
- USDC in an Euler vault on Arbitrum

The system may assign bonuses to:

- Low-risk strategies such as large money-market pools
- Opportunities on liquid chains (Arbitrum, Base, Ethereum)
- Opportunities denominated in ETH or USDC
- Opportunities offering higher realised APR than the current vault
- Opportunities with active reward campaigns

The highest-scoring opportunities appear as featured recommendations or display a special **handpicked** indicator.

## What is the One-Click Deposit experience?

**The One-Click Deposit is Jumper Earn's cross-chain, single-transaction investment flow.**

It allows users to deposit into a vault:

- From any token
- From any chain
- With any wallet (EOA or smart wallet)
- In one click
- Without paying gas directly

## How does the One-Click Zap work?

The flow is powered by:

- LI.FI's Composer & cross-chain routing

### Execution process

1. Funds move from the user's wallet into a dedicated smart wallet (one per user).
2. All swap, bridge, and deposit steps are batched into a single calldata instruction.
3. The smart wallet executes the transaction.
4. The resulting vault tokens are sent back to the user's wallet automatically.

The user experiences this as a single seamless action.
