## What is Jumper Missions?

Jumper Missions is Jumper's mission and campaign hub where users can discover new products and ecosystems, complete onchain and off-chain tasks, and earn rewards and Jumper Pass XP.

It connects exploration, participation, and loyalty into a single structured experience inside the Jumper Smart Money app.

## Completing and verifying missions

In order to complete a mission, users must successfully complete **all required mission steps** and pass the associated verification checks.

Each mission includes a defined set of requirements, which may consist of:

- On-chain transactions
- Swap, bridge, or Earn actions
- Portfolio interactions
- Off-chain or partner-defined tasks

A mission is only marked as **completed** once **every requirement has been fulfilled and verified**.
