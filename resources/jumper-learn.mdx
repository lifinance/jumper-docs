## What is Jumper Learn?

Jumper Learn is Jumper's education hub designed to help users understand DeFi concepts, products, and ecosystems through clear, structured, and accessible learning content.

It enables users to learn how DeFi works before, during, and after interacting with the Jumper platform.

Learn more about Jumper through chains and tools deep-dive, monthly reports on [Jumper Learn](https://jumper.exchange/learn)
