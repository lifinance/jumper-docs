Jumper's Same-Chain Swaps use a **MetaDEX Aggregator** to source liquidity from multiple decentralized exchanges on a single blockchain, ensuring users receive optimized pricing, liquidity access, and execution quality.

Instead of relying on a single DEX, Jumper intelligently compares routes across leading decentralized exchanges to deliver the best possible swap outcome on the same chain.

## What the MetaDEX Aggregator Does

- Sources liquidity from multiple DEXs
- Optimizes for price, liquidity, and slippage
- Executes swaps through the most efficient route
- Offers gasless swaps

This allows users to swap tokens on the same blockchain with better execution than using a single exchange.
