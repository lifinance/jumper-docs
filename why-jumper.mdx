Jumper has expanded the platform from best-in-class cross-chain swaps into a full experience for earning, perks, and portfolio management—without adding friction for existing swap users.

## What’s new

- **Jumper Earn:** Personalized yield discovery with a recommendation engine tuned to your wallet holdings and chain preferences. Zap from any supported token into curated pools in one flow.
- **Zap flows:** Bridge \+ swap \+ deposit in one path to land in the target pool. Minimize hops, approvals, and failed routes.
- **Jumper Perks:** Benefits tied to your Jumper Pass level, unlocking partner rewards as you climb the levels.
- **Jumper Portfolio:** View balances and positions, rebalance, and rotate yields without leaving Jumper.
- **Advanced (coming soon):** Get advanced functionality for your crypto trades alongside current features.

## Who it’s for

- **Power swappers:** Turn existing swap behavior into yield entry with minimal extra clicks.
- **Yield seekers:** Curated, risk-aware pools across chains with clear metrics and zap entry/exit.
- **New to yield:** Starter-friendly routes matched to wallet size and risk tolerance.

## Why it’s different

- **Optimal routing:** Compare quotes, fees, and execution time before confirming transaction.
- **Personalized recommendations:** Earn goes beyond APR lists by ranking pools based on your assets and chain preferences.
- **One-click cross-chain zaps:** Start from any supported token on any chain and arrive in the target pool in one flow.
- **Unified experience:** Enter, track, rebalance, and exit from the same interface that already powers swaps and bridges.
- **Cross-chain breadth:** Built on LI.FI rails to reach the best routes across supported ecosystems.

## How the Earn flow works

1. **Get a quote:** Jumper finds the optimal cross-chain path (bridge \+ DEX) to reach the target pool.
2. **Zap to earn:** Approvals and routing are handled in one flow; you sign with your wallet.
3. **Track and rebalance:** Use Portfolio to monitor positions and move between pools as conditions change.

## Perks and loyalty

Jumper Pass ties onchain activity to perks. Higher tiers unlock more partner benefits. Perks are shown contextually so you see value while you transact.
