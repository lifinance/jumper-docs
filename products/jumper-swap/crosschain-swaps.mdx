Jumper enables **crosschain swaps**, allowing users to swap a token on one blockchain into a token on another blockchain in a single, unified flow.

Jumper is an aggregation and routing interface that selects the optimal route across multiple liquidity sources and bridge providers.

<Note>
Jumper crosschain swaps combine bridging and swapping into one automated execution experience.
</Note>

## Is Jumper a bridge?

No.

Jumper is not a bridge. Jumper is an aggregation and routing interface that uses multiple underlying bridge and liquidity providers to execute crosschain swaps.

Jumper is the most complete crosschain swap and bridge interface in the whole ecosystem.
