### How it works

1. Discover: Jumper scans your wallet, detects idle assets and current positions, and scores opportunities based on relevance. The _For You_ feed shows the curated opportunities. 
2. Zap to earn: Pick an opportunity in _For You_ or _All Markets_ and deposit in one click — from any token, any chain. Routing, swaps, and approvals are handled in a single flow.
3. Track and rebalance: Monitor positions in Portfolio and rotate between pools as conditions change.

### Personalized discovery engine

Each time you visit Earn, the engine scans your portfolio and scores every opportunity against multiple factors:

- Risk profile alignment: favors strategies matching your current behavior.
- Chain liquidity: bonuses for opportunities on liquid chains you already use.
- Asset compatibility: bonuses for opportunities denominated in tokens you hold.
- Relative yield: bonuses for opportunities offering higher APR than your current positions.
- Active rewards: bonuses for opportunities with live incentive campaigns.

The highest-scoring opportunities surface as featured recommendations with a "handpicked" indicator.\

Example: You hold ETH on Base and USDC in an Euler vault on Arbitrum. The engine assigns bonuses to low-risk strategies, opportunities on Arbitrum/Base/Ethereum, ETH or USDC-denominated pools, pools beating your current vault's APR, and pools with active reward campaigns.

### Advanced search

Beyond personalized recommendations, you can also navigate to All Markets and search through all listed opportunities. Users can filter and sort opportunities by:

- underlying asset, 
- blockchain, 
- protocol category (money market, yield aggregator, LSD, etc.), 
- protocol name, 
- 30-day APR, and
- TVL.

### One-click zap

The one-click deposit flow lets you enter any vault from any token on any chain in a single transaction:

1. Funds move from your wallet into a dedicated smart wallet (one per user).
2. All swap, bridge, and deposit steps are batched into a single instruction.
3. The smart wallet executes the transaction.
4. Vault tokens are returned to your wallet automatically.

This is powered by [LI.FI](http://LI.FI)'s Composer and cross-chain routing.

### Connection to other products

Earn receives capital from Swap (same-chain and cross-chain). Positions created in Earn are tracked in Portfolio. Earn activity earns XP toward your Jumper Pass.
