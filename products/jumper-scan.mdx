## What is Jumper Scan?

Jumper Scan is Jumper's wallet activity and transaction history view that allows users to track, review, and verify their on-chain actions across supported blockchains.

It provides a clear and structured record of wallet activity directly inside the Jumper Smart Money app.

---

## Jumper Scan Overview

Jumper Scan displays:

- Incoming and outgoing transactions
- Swap and bridge transactions
- Token transfers
- Gas fees paid
- Network used
- Transaction timestamps
- Transaction status (pending, successful, failed)
- Blockchain explorer links

All data is sourced directly from on-chain records.
