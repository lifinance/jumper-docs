Jumper is a B2C app for swapping, bridging, earning yield, and collecting perks—no APIs or developer setup required.

## Step 1: Connect your wallet

- Open Jumper and select **Connect wallet**.
- Choose your provider (e.g., MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect).
- Approve the connection in your wallet.

## Step 2: Pick what you want to do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shuffle" title="Swap (same chain)">
    Select From chain/token, To token, enter an amount, review the estimate, then confirm. Some tokens need an approval first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="arrow-right-arrow-left" title="Bridge (cross-chain)">
    Select From chain/token and To chain/token, review output, time, and fees, then confirm and track progress.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="sparkles" title="Earn and perks">
    Visit Jumper 2.0 to see personalized yield options and perks tied to your Jumper Pass tier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="trophy" title="Missions">
    Complete missions to earn points and unlock additional benefits. Check the missions panel for active tasks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="fuel-pump" title="Gas Top Up">
    If you arrive on a new chain with no native gas, top up a small amount so you can act immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Review and confirm

- Check route details, estimated output, expected time, and known fees.
- Keep the slippage setting appropriate: tighter for price protection, wider for higher fill probability.
- Confirm in your wallet. Stay on the tracking screen for multi-step routes.

## Step 4: Track status

- Watch the in-app tracker for pending, bridging, and completed states.
- If pending, check your wallet activity or the block explorer.

## Tips for smooth usage

- **Approvals:** Some tokens require a one-time approval. Approve only what's needed when possible.
- **Gas:** You pay gas on the chain where you sign. Multi-step routes can need gas for more than one transaction.
- **Safety:** Double check From/To chains and tokens; bookmark the official Jumper domain; start with small tests on new chains.
- **Stay updated:** Check `https://status.li.fi` if routes look slow or paused.
