Keep these tips in mind when using Jumper.

## Slippage and pricing

- Set slippage to balance reliability and price. Tight slippage may fail more; wider slippage may fill more but can reduce output.
  <Frame>
    ![Screenshot 2026 07 09 At 6 15 34 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-07-09-at-6.15.34-PM.png)
  </Frame>
- Watch estimated output and price impact when liquidity is thin.

## Approvals

- Some tokens require approval before swapping. Approval is a separate transaction.
- Prefer limited approvals when your wallet supports it.

## Gas and fees

- You pay gas on the chain where you sign. Multi-step routes can need gas for more than one step.
- Review expected gas and any provider fees before confirming.

## Status and tracking

- Stay on the tracking screen until completion, especially for bridges.
  <Frame>
    ![Screenshot 2026 07 09 At 6 17 00 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-07-09-at-6.17.00-PM.png)
  </Frame>
- If a transaction is pending, check your wallet activity or the block explorer.

## Safety reminders

- Double-check From/To chains and tokens before confirming.
- Bookmark the official Jumper domain to avoid fake sites.
- Start with a small test amount on new chains or tokens.
