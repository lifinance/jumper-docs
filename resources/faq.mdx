## 2. How to change the receiving address

By default, Jumper sends funds to the wallet that started the swap. If you're bridging to a non-EVM chain, you'll need to paste an address that supports that network.

To send funds to a different wallet, click the **wallet icon** next to the Exchange button.

<Frame>
  ![4](/images/4.gif)
</Frame>

Your swap summary will now show the updated destination wallet.

---

## 3. How to find your transaction details

After your transaction completes, click the **link button** in the swap overview to open Jumper Scan with your transaction details.

<Frame>
  ![5](/images/5.gif)
</Frame>

To see all your activity, go to [jumper.xyz/scan](http://jumper.xyz/scan) and paste your wallet address. This shows your full transaction history.

Click any transaction to see the full breakdown: transaction hash, bridge used, gas fees, tokens sent and received, source and destination addresses, and blockchain explorer links.
