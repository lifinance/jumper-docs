## 1. How to find your transaction hash

Learn how to use Jumper to find your transaction hash and use Jumper Scan.

When your swap is finished you can click on the "link button" underneath the Transaction ID tab of your swap overview to be redirected to Jumper Scan to find all the info in your swap.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (14).png" alt="Transaction link button" />
</Frame>

If you only have your wallet address, you can head over to [https://jumper.exchange/scan](https://jumper.exchange/scan) and paste in your wallet address. This will allow you to see all the transactions your wallet has made via Jumper.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (15).png" alt="Jumper Scan search" />
</Frame>

Once you find your transaction, you can click on it to reveal all the details about the swap:

- Transaction hash
- Bridge used
- Gas fees
- Token sent
- Token received
- Source/From address
- Destination/To address
- Blockchain Explorer Links

## 2. How do I change the destination/To address?

If you'd like to change the wallet that receives the funds for your swap, you can click on the "Wallet icon" which can be found next to the "Exchange" Button on Jumper.

### Step 1: Select source token

First, select the chain and token you wish to bridge from:

<Frame>

Next, select the chain and token you want to swap to.

<Frame>

Next, input the amount you'd like to swap.

## How does the Quick Deposit work?

The **Quick Deposit** feature significantly improves the user experience by allowing users to deposit into Jumper Earn opportunities **from almost any supported asset on any supported chain**, without needing to manually perform multiple steps themselves.

Behind the scenes, a Quick Deposit is a multi-step flow, which depends on your starting asset and chain. These can include:

- A token swap
- A cross-chain bridge
- A token approval (for the selected pool)
- The final deposit transaction into the yield opportunity

### Quick Deposit Flow

#### 1. Select Asset and Chain

Selects the token and chain they currently hold funds on. Jumper then displays an estimate of what the user will receive in the selected pool once all steps are completed.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (33).png" alt="Asset selection" />
</Frame>

#### 2. Sign Required Transactions

You need to sign each required transaction as prompted by their wallet. This may include approvals, swaps, bridging, and the final deposit.

#### 3. Deposit Completion

Once all transactions have successfully executed on-chain, the funds are deposited into the selected yield opportunity and will appear on the "your positions" tab on the main Jumper Earn page and in their Jumper Portfolio

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (34).png" alt="Deposit complete" />
</Frame>

## How to Remove Funds From a Position

Withdrawing funds from a yield opportunity on **Jumper Earn** follows a **multi-step on-chain process**, similar to the Quick Deposit flow.

### Where You Can View Your Positions

You can access your active Earn positions from:

- The **Jumper Earn page**, under the **"Your Positions"** section
- **Jumper Portfolio**, under the **"DeFi Protocols"** section which displays deposits made via Jumper Earn

**Jumper Earn View:**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (35).png" alt="Earn positions view" />
</Frame>

**Jumper Portfolio View:**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (36).png" alt="Portfolio positions view" />
</Frame>

### Withdrawal Flow

#### Step 1: Initiate Withdrawal

Click Withdraw on the relevant active position.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (37).png" alt="Withdraw button" />
</Frame>

#### Step 2: Select Destination Token and Chain

You will need to select:

- The token they want to receive
- The chain they want the funds withdrawn to

An estimated output amount is displayed before proceeding.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image (38).png" alt="Withdrawal destination" />
</Frame>

#### Step 3: Sign Required Transactions

You need to sign all required transactions as prompted by your wallet.

These steps may include:

- Withdrawing funds from the underlying protocol
- Token approvals (if required)
- Swaps and/or bridging transactions

#### Step 4: Withdrawal Completion

Once all transactions have successfully completed on-chain, you will receive the selected token on the chosen chain.

The position will update in Jumper Earn and Jumper Portfolio after a few moments.
