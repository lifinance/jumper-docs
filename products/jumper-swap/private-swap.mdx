## What is Private Swap?

Private Swap on Jumper allows you to swap and bridge assets across 15\+ chains with no observable onchain link between sender and receiver. 

Powered by Houdini Swap, these transactions process in a few seconds to a few minutes while keeping your wallet shielded from MEV bots, trackers, and copy-traders.
