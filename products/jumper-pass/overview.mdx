## What is Jumper Pass

Jumper Pass is Jumper's rewards program that unlocks multiple benefits for users when they engage with the Jumper ecosystem.

It connects onchain activity to real utility by unlocking digital and real-world benefits, partner discounts, and other rewards across the Jumper platform.

It tracks user engagement across Jumper products and unlocks access to:

- Partner perks and rewards
- Campaign participation
- Future ecosystem benefits
- Community-based incentives

Jumper Pass transforms usage into long-term value.

---

## How Jumper Pass Works

When users interact with Jumper:

- Swapping tokens
- Bridging across chains
- Using Earn

Their activity contributes toward Jumper Pass participation by rewarding users in XP, causing them to climb up the Jumper Pass ranks.

Jumper Pass then determines eligibility for perks, campaigns, and future benefits based on users ranking, engagement criteria defined by Jumper and its partners.

---

## What Jumper Pass Unlocks

Jumper Pass may provide access to:

- Digital rewards
- Partner discounts
- Early access to product features
- Campaign participation
- Event access
- Community incentives

Available perks depend on region, campaign, and partner availability.

---

## Why Jumper Pass Exists

DeFi usage today provides little long-term user recognition.

Jumper Pass introduces:

- Persistent user identity across DeFi activity
- Long-term reward alignment
- Ecosystem participation incentives
- A bridge between on-chain activity and real utility

Jumper Pass aligns user growth with ecosystem growth.

## Who Should Use Jumper Pass?

Jumper Pass is designed for users who:

- Actively use DeFi
- Want long-term ecosystem benefits
- Participate in campaigns
- Prefer value-aligned loyalty programs
