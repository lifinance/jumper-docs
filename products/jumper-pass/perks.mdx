## What are Jumper Perks?

**Jumper Perks are benefits and advantages unlocked by Jumper users based on their Jumper Pass level.**

Users can unlock up to **25+ on-chain and off-chain perks** by increasing their XP through activity in the Jumper ecosystem.

Perks may include partner rewards, platform benefits, early access features, and community advantages. Perk availability depends on the user's current Jumper Pass level and may change over time.
