Connect a wallet, swap or bridge tokens, and start earning in no time with Jumper. 

## Step 1: Connect your wallet

- Open [<u>jumper.xyz</u>](http://jumper.xyz) and click Connect wallet.
- Choose your wallet provider (MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.).
- Approve the connection in your wallet.

## Step 2: Pick what you want to do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Swap (same chain)" icon="shuffle">
    Go to [<u>jumper.xyz</u>](http://jumper.xyz) and select the assets you want to swap, enter the amount, review the quotes, select your preferred rate. If you’re swapping a token for the first time, it may need an approval transaction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bridge (cross-chain)" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Select your assets for source and destination chains. Enter the amount you want to move and review the quotes, estimated time, and fees. 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Swap + Bridge combined" icon="arrow-progress">
    This is the most common Jumper flow. Example: start with USDC on Arbitrum and receive ETH on Base. Jumper handles the swap and bridge as one transaction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Private Swap" icon="eye-slash">
    Go to [jumper.xyz/private](https://jumper.xyz/private) and select the asset you want to swap, your destination chain, asset, and wallet address. 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Earn" icon="money-bill-trend-up">
    Click on Earn to go to [jumper.xyz/earn](https://jumper.xyz/earn). Select a personalized earning opportunity or choose one under the "All Markets" tab. Choose the asset and chain you want to deposit from.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Gas Top Up" icon="gas-pump">
    If you're moving to a new chain and need the gas token, go to [jumper.xyz/gas](https://jumper.xyz/gas) and select any token on any supported chain to get gas.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Review and confirm

- Check route details, estimated output, expected time, and quoted fees.
- Keep the slippage setting appropriate: tighter for price protection, wider for higher fill probability.
- Confirm in your wallet. Stay on the tracking screen for multi-step routes.

## Step 4: Track status

- Watch the in-app tracker for pending, bridging, and completed states.
- If pending, check your wallet activity or the block explorer.
