Jumper’s swap and bridge interface lets you trade tokens on the same chain or across chains from a single interface. It finds the best possible route for the asset and amount you want to swap or bridge by comparing quotes from integrated platforms for the best price, speed, and reliability.

## How it works

1. Select a source token (any chain) and a destination token (same or different chain).
2. Jumper computes the optimal route across liquidity and bridge providers.
3. Review the quote: estimated output, time, fees, and price impact.
4. Confirm in your wallet. Funds arrive as the destination token and chain.

## Gas Top Up

Landing on a new chain with no native gas? Gas Top Up adds a small amount of the destination chain's native token so you can transact immediately. Start small and you can add more later if you plan multiple actions.

---

## Key features

- Route aggregation: Compares routes across all integrated providers.
- Gasless swaps: Available on supported chains and pairs.
- Slippage controls: Adjust tolerance in settings (see Glossary: Slippage).
- Gas estimation: Shows expected gas cost before you confirm.
- Quote transparency: See exactly what you'll receive, how long it takes, and what fees apply.
- Interop Token Standards: Supports interop-compatible tokens.

---

## Fees and pricing

- Network gas fees — Paid to the blockchain, not to Jumper.
- Provider fees — May apply depending on the route.
- Price impact — Thin liquidity can reduce output.

The review screen shows all costs alongside the estimated output, time, known fees, and expected gas before you confirm.

## Connection to other products

Swap and Bridge move capital. Your capital can flow into Earn positions and is tracked in Portfolio. Swap activity earns XP toward your Jumper Pass.
